Official Game Guide

1. Radical Score Logic

Your session score is automatically converted into its Simplified Radical Form. For example, a score of 12 displays as 2√3. This is purely visual and does not affect your math!

2. Progression & Power

Your Click Power scales based on your Total Career Clicks:

  • Bronze: 0 - 999 clicks (+1 Power)
  • Silver: 1,000 - 9,999 clicks (+10 Power)
  • Gold: 10,000+ clicks (+20 Power)

3. The Auto-Clicker Ability

Unlock the Ultimate Auto-Clicker by reaching 100 Billion All-Time clicks. Once active, it generates 10 Clicks Per Second (CPS) for 60 seconds. Cooldown: 10 Minutes.

4. Stealth & Safety

Boss Mode: Press 'B' to instantly hide the game behind math notes. This also mutes all audio.

Manual Cancel: Clicking the manual button while the Auto-Clicker is active will immediately abort the session and start the cooldown.

5. Inactivity Rules

To keep the Auto-Clicker unlocked, you must remain active. If you do not click for 48 hours, a warning appears. At 72 hours, the ability locks.
